#EducationForAll

Thanks to your purchases, YouGoatMe supports projects for access to education for young girls in Morocco.

YouGoatMe partnered with the UK-based non-profit EducationForAll (EFA) to build and run high-quality boarding houses for girls from 12-18. Everything is provided for free (3 nutritious meals a day, hot showers, cosy beds, access to computers, study support via an international volunteer program and dedicated, local housemothers).

The overall environment makes it easy for the girls to settle in and thrive in their studies.

For each merchandise you buy at YouGoatMe, we donate $6 enough to cover expenses for 3 days for a girl.

We set a goal of $10,000 to raise for 2018. That would provide one year of free boarding school to 14 girls.
